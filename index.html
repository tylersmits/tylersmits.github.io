Meacham's Location

Meacham is located 5 kms from the junction of highways 2 and 5. It is approximately 30 minutes east of Saskatoon (a large city) with an international airport. It is 15 minutes from Colonsay and the same to Viscount.

Birds and Wildlife

Meacham is on the flight route for numerous migratory birds and most of the year is alive with the sound of songbirds, huge flocks of geese and cranes (to name a few). There are also deer, fox, coyotes, beaver, porcupines and a wide range of other mammals living in the area but rarely in town.

Geophysical Overview

Meacham is surrounded by farmland and shares the climate and weather conditions of the central region of Saskatchewan. The village is situated in a shallow valley, protecting it slightly from wind. The air quality is excellent and the community enjoys amazing views of sunsets, sunrises, thunderstorms and northern lights.

Quality of Life

Meacham has a high quality of living often described as peaceful and quiet with a healthy natural environment, kindness of neighbours, safety, and proximity to services. Social events are well attended and there is a thriving arts community.
