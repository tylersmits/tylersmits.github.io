Meacham Businesses and Services

Meacham has a Canada Post Office, a Village Office,

a SGI agent/motor license issuer and fire hall staffed by volunteers.

Additional businesses include: a hotel; a gift shop and art gallery; a part-time beauty salon; a consulting company; a dinner theatre; a small graphics company and several pottery and artist production studios. As well, there is a trucking company, a heavy equipment road construction business, an auction service, a seed cleaning company and a bulk fuel outlet.
