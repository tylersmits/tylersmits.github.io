Meacham Business & Development

Room For Opportunity To Grow

BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT
Meacham may have a smaller population, but offers several benefits to a small business owner.
Our town property tax rate does not vary for commercial vs residential land use. So your business would pay no more property taxes than if it were your home.
Generous lot sizes means space to grow.
Nygren Accounting Services offers local small to large business accounting services right here in town.
As a small rural village, Meacham supports local above all else. We will help you succeed as a community
