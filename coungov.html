COUNCIL MEETINGS
The Meacham village council meets monthly. Meeting dates are posted both here, and on our village Facebook page. Meetings are open to the public to observe, but currently masks are required and social distancing is enforced. Villagers wishing to speak on any issue can obtain a delegate form HERE; which must be printed, filled out, and delivered to the village office by either the Tuesday or Thursday before the meeting. Villagers can then appear in-person to have their concerns discussed for the record.

PROPERTY TAXES
Property taxes are due by December 31st, and the village office will always be open on the last working day of the year. Property tax assessments are sent out in April and there is a discount of up to 4% for early payment!
Payment can be made in three ways.
Payments are accepted via cash, or cheque, in-person during normal village office hours.
Payments can be made through an Affinity Credit Union account using Village Of Meacham as payee.
Your account ID is the first four letters of your last name, the first three letters of your first name, and the letter T.
Example: John Smith = SMITJOHT
Payments can also be made via Telpay service

WATER BILLS
Water bills for the village are generated quarterly, and payment is due within 28 days. The base charge includes four thousand gallons per quarter, and any usage over and above is billed by the thousand gallons, or portion thereof, usage at time of reporting.
Payment can be made in three ways.
Payments are accepted via cash, or cheque, in-person during normal village office hours.
Payments can be made through an Affinity Credit Union account using Village Of Meacham as payee.
Your account ID is the first four letters of your last name, the first three letters of your first name, and the letter U.
Example: John Smith = SMITJOHU
Payments can also be made via Telpay service
