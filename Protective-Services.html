Village of Meacham Protective Services

The RCMP detachment serving Meacham is part of the Saskatoon detachment and is physically located in Colonsay. Ambulance services are also Saskatoon based.

Fire protection is provided by a volunteer, trained (but not certified) fire fighter group. >> 10 firefighters, 2 first reposnders, 2 fire trucks (1 equipped with foaming unit for grass fires).
